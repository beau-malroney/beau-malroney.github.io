## Berserk: All Series and Adaptations

Berserk is one of the most influential dark fantasy manga series, created by Kentaro Miura. Since its debut in 1989, Berserk has inspired numerous anime adaptations, films, games, and spin-off content.

### Manga

- **Berserk (ベルセルク)**
  - Written and illustrated by Kentaro Miura.
  - Serialization began in 1989 and continues as of 2025.
  - The manga is collected in 43+ volumes.

### Anime Series

- **Berserk (1997)**
  - 25-episode TV anime covering the Golden Age Arc.

- **Berserk (2016 & 2017)**
  - Two seasons, continuing the story with the Conviction Arc and the start of the Falcon of the Millennium Empire Arc.

### Film Trilogy

- **Berserk: The Golden Age Arc**
  - **The Egg of the King (2012)**
  - **The Battle for Doldrey (2012)**
  - **The Advent (2013)**

- **Berserk: The Golden Age Arc – Memorial Edition (2022)**
  - Remastered version of the film trilogy re-edited into 13 TV episodes with new scenes.

### Other Media

- **Video Games**
  - *Sword of the Berserk: Guts’ Rage* (Dreamcast, 1999/2000)
  - *Berserk and the Band of the Hawk* (2016)
  - *Millennium Falcon Hen Seima Senki no Sho* (Japan-exclusive)

- **Books & Collectibles**
  - Official art books, novels, and a trading card game.

---

The Berserk saga continues to attract new fans with its compelling storytelling, iconic characters, and dark, detailed world. Whether you’re a longtime reader or new to the franchise, there’s a wealth of content to explore across different media.
