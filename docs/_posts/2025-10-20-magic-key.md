## Disneyland California Annual Pass vs. Single Tickets – 2025 Cost Comparison

### Ticket Prices (2025)
- **1-Day, 1-Park:** $104–$224 (tiered, depending on date)
- **1-Day Park Hopper:** $174–$314
- **Multi-Day Park Hopper:**
  - 2-Day: $435
  - 3-Day: $535
  - 4-Day: $600
  - 5-Day: $655

### Magic Key (Annual Pass) Prices
- **Inspire Key:** $1,899 (no blockouts, includes parking, best perks)
- **Believe Key:** $1,474 (some blockouts, 50% off parking)
- **Enchant Key:** $974 (more blockouts)
- **Imagine Key:** $599 (SoCal residents only, extensive blockouts)

---

### Break-Even Days (Out-of-State Visitors, Park Hopper)
- **Inspire Key:** About **11 days** (using regular pricing)[134]
- **Believe Key:** About **9 days**
- **Enchant Key:** About **6 days**
- **Imagine Key:** About **4 days** (SoCal residents only)

---

#### Sample Scenario: Two 4-Day Trips (8 Days)
- **4-Day Park Hopper:** $600 × 2 = $1,200
- **Inspire Key:** $1,899
  - Additional perks: free parking ($35/day), 20% off merch, 15% off food, free PhotoPass, special events

#### Other Perks to Consider:
- **Parking:** $35/day included with Inspire Key (worth $280 for 8 days)
- **Park Hopper**: always included with annual passes
- **Merch/Food Discount:** up to 20% off
- **PhotoPass:** included at higher tiers
- **Special Events/Previews:** exclusive for passholders

---

### Summary
- The value of a Disneyland annual pass is maximized for frequent visitors (typically **9–11+ days/year** with Park Hopper).
- Additional perks (free parking, food/merch discounts, PhotoPass) make it more appealing if you plan extra trips or use the discounts.
- Blockout dates vary—check calendars before buying!
- Most non-local families break even at **11 days** for the Inspire Key, or **9 days** for the Believe Key, with all perks considered.
