## How to Evolve Inkay in Pokémon Z-A

Inkay is a unique Dark/Psychic-type Pokémon known for its special evolution method. Traditionally, evolving Inkay involves an unusual twist on handheld controls—here’s what to do in Pokémon Z-A.

### Steps to Evolve Inkay

- Level Up Inkay to Level 30 or above.
- Physically flip your device (Nintendo Switch/Nintendo Switch 2) upside down
- Navigate to the menu
- Select Inkay
- Hit the A Button
- Select evolve from the menu (All done while device is upside down.)
